Our story

Started in the garage. Earned its way out.

Precision Tech Solutions began the way most good workshops do — one technician, a borrowed unit, and a customer who needed something installed properly. That first install became a second. The second became fifty. Eight years later, we're a multi-location installation company quietly responsible for thousands of hours of clean, hidden, factory-grade wiring in vehicles across two countries.

We don't sell flashy. We sell predictable. The dashcam that records every trip without you ever touching it. The GPS unit that's still reporting five years from now. The wiring run that the next mechanic looks at and assumes was done at the factory.

"If a customer ever has to think about the install we did, we did it wrong."

That's the standard we built the company on, and it's the standard every technician working under our name is trained to meet.
